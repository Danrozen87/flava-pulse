# Research Agent 5: SEO Trends, Pain Points & Opportunities

## Inherits
All rules from `research-protocol.md` apply.

## Scope
- What restaurant managers search for RIGHT NOW
- Franchise owner discussions and pain points
- Reddit, forums, industry community sentiment
- Google Trends for restaurant-related queries
- AI chatbot trends in food/restaurant context
- Marketing trends restaurants adopt
- Operational pain points accumulating in the industry
- Staff management, cost control, tech adoption barriers

## Source Registry Categories
- `seo_painpoints` (primary)
- Cross-reference with `hospitality_*` for market context

## Search Queries (Temporal)
Run with current date context:

1. "restaurant manager challenges [year]" — operational pain
2. "franchise owner technology [year]" — franchise perspective
3. "Reddit restaurantowners [month year]" — community sentiment
4. "restaurant staff shortage [year] [region]" — labor market
5. "restaurant marketing trends [year]" — go-to-market
6. "restaurant upselling technology [year]" — revenue optimization
7. "restaurant menu personalization demand [year]" — market pull
8. "restaurant technology adoption barrier [year]" — friction points
9. "restaurant AI chatbot ordering [year]" — AI interaction trends
10. "restaurant customer experience technology [year]" — guest experience
11. "restaurant cost management [year]" — financial pressure
12. "Google Trends restaurant [region] [year]" — search behavior

## Priority Signals
Flag immediately:
- Spike in search volume for "menu personalization" or "AI restaurant"
- Recurring complaints about POS systems on Reddit/forums
- New pain points appearing (e.g., new regulation compliance burden)
- Marketing trends that align with Flava's positioning
- Franchise owners expressing interest in AI tools

## Sentiment Analysis
For Reddit/forum findings, capture:
- **Positive**: What are people excited about?
- **Negative**: What are people frustrated with?
- **Wish list**: What do people want that doesn't exist yet?
- **Skeptical**: What are people doubtful about?

## 5 Whys Focus
Apply to: Recurring pain points (if the same issue appears 3+ times, drill into root cause).

## Cost-Benefit Focus
Apply to: Pain points that Flava directly solves — quantify the value.
