## Priority Signals
Flag immediately:
- Regulatory changes affecting restaurants (allergens, accessibility, labor law)
- Major chain expansions or contractions in target markets
- Restaurant tech adoption statistics (new surveys, reports)
- Consumer spending trend reversals
- Visita, DEHOGA, or NRA major reports
